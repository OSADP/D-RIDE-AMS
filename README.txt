Open Source Overview
============================
D-RIDE AMS consist of the source-code and the compiled application used as part of the AMS (Analysis, Modeling and Simulation Testbeds for DMA and ATDM Evaluation) Phoenix Testbed. This Dynamic Mobility Application is used to simulate real world enabeling of drivers and passengers to dynamically identify and accept potential ridesharing opportunities along a given travel route. 

D-RIDE stands for Dynamic Ridesharing.



For further details on the applications, users are encouraged to read the following FHWA documents:
